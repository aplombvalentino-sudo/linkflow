# LinkFlow — Landing Wireframe + Copy (Phase 4/5)

Scroll model: the page is a 5-chapter film scrubbed by scroll. Progress = 2px volt line
under the floating nav. Scene index chips ("01 / 05", Space Mono) anchor each chapter.
Chapter accents interpolate: violet → mono/gray → volt → ember → volt-on-ink.

## 01 — Hero "stage" (pinned, ~260vh scroll budget)
- Layout: left = headline block; right = glass phone playing the fitness-creator demo profile.
- Scrub timeline: phone rises + tilts from 12°→0°; headline words stagger-in with slight
  rotation; violet radial glow expands; at 60% the demo profile's link tiles cascade in;
  at 85% headline block yields to proof line.
- Copy: h1 "Your link is a stage." · sub "LinkFlow turns the link in your bio into an
  animated, on-brand profile — with analytics you'll actually read." · CTA [Claim your handle]
  (volt pill, magnetic) · ghost [See it move ↓] · proof line "Free forever · live in 60 seconds".
- Nav: logo wordmark · Log in (ghost) · Claim your handle (volt pill).

## 02 — "Why not Linktree?" (pinned horizontal track, 3 panels, ~300vh)
Vertical scroll drives translateX. Chapter opens desaturated (the gray world), ends re-lit.
- Panel A: simplified generic bio page mock (white bg, gray buttons) — "Every bio link
  looks the same." / "White page. Gray buttons. Your brand, flattened."
- Panel B: split contrast — "You post cinematic content." / "Then send people to a beige list."
- Panel C: the SAME links morph into LinkFlow volt theme (crossfade+lift) — "Same links.
  Different gravity." · mini-CTA [Make the switch].

## 03 — Features in motion (4 story beats, alternating, viewport-entry animations)
Each beat: mono chip · h3 · ≤2 lines · one animated visual. No card grid.
1. Multi-profile — "One account. Every persona." / "Your fitness brand, your podcast, your
   side project — separate stages, one login." Visual: 3 stacked glass profile cards fanning.
2. Analytics — "Know what they tap." / "Views, clicks, CTR per link. Numbers with taste —
   no dashboard soup." Visual: analytics card with live-counting mono numerals + sparkline.
3. Themes — "Motion is your brand color." / "Three cinematic themes tuned for 60fps. Pick
   one; stay recognizable." Visual: theme swatch flips volt→violet→ember on the mini profile.
4. QR — "From stage to street." / "A themed QR code for posters, merch, gym walls." Visual:
   QR tile draws itself (stroke animation).

## 04 — Creator proof (scroll-scrubbed reel, ~200vh)
- 3 demo profiles (fitness coach @maera.fit, streamer @novaplays, artist @june.waves) slide,
  rotate ≤4°, fade as scroll scrubs; ember gradient shift. Labeled "demo profiles" (honesty
  rule — no fabricated testimonials).
- Copy: "Built for the ones being watched." / "Claim it before someone takes @you."

## 04b — Pricing strip (compact, static reveal)
- Two glass columns only. Free: 1 profile, unlimited links, 7-day analytics. Pro €9/mo:
  10 profiles, all themes, full history, no badge. CTA on Pro only [Go Pro]. One line
  under: "Start free. Upgrade when you outgrow one stage."

## 05 — Final CTA (clean, high-contrast, minimal motion)
- Pure ink, one h2 "The stage is yours.", volt CTA [Claim your handle], subtle glow pulse
  (4s loop), mono line "Free forever · no card · 60 seconds". Footer: minimal (logo, legal
  links, © 2026).

## V1 self-critique → V2 changes (Phase 4 loop)
1. V1 hero had CTA + ghost + proof + nav CTA = 3 volt elements above fold → demoted ghost
   to text link, nav CTA becomes glass until hero CTA scrolls away (single dominant CTA rule).
2. V1 features beat 4 visuals were all "card with icon" — beat 2/4 redesigned to live
   counter + self-drawing QR so no two beats share a visual pattern.
3. V1 horizontal track panels were text-on-glass boxes (box+text slop) → each panel now
   contains a working mini profile mock, copy ≤2 lines.
4. V1 proof scene used testimonial quotes with invented names/photos — violates honesty
   rule → replaced with clearly-labeled demo profiles.
5. Pricing was a 3-tier matrix → collapsed to Free/Pro two-column strip.
